Brickasso Registry: #02

Pepsi – LEGO® 3D Mosaic

Born between two worlds, Italy and Romania. I created “Pepsi” as a bridge between precision and passion. Inspired by the red and blue bezel collectors call “Pepsi,” this mosaic transforms an icon of design into a sculptural dialogue between color, geometry, and rhythm.

Each LEGO® element is placed with watchmaker discipline, creating micro-gradients and shadow depth that shift with the light. Set on a Novacolor ARCHI+ Big surface with Metallo Fuso finish and framed in solid wood, the piece reflects both my cultural duality and the universal language of craft.

Recorded as BRK-02 in the Brickasso Registry.

Brickasso is an independent artistic reinterpretation and is not affiliated with, nor endorsed or sponsored by any watch brand.
