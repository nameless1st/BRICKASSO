Afghan Girl – LEGO® 3D Mosaic

A portrait about gaze and human dignity. Built from tens of thousands of LEGO® elements, the image keeps a quiet gravity and sculptural depth.

Colours are paced to carry breath and memory across the surface, while the mosaic structure seeks resonance rather than imitation.
