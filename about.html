About the Artist

I’m Albert Oprea — I was 10 years old when I launched this website in 2025. Maybe one day I’ll come back and update this line. But the story behind Brickasso stays the same.

I started building LEGO® sets when I was just 4 years old. After assembling and disassembling hundreds of sets, I was left with bags full of bricks. Instead of throwing them away, I began creating something new — from my own imagination.

Inspired by my parents’ passion for watches, I built my first LEGO mosaic of a Rolex. From there, one brick led to another… one image led to a new idea… and a small hobby turned into something much greater.

I’ve recreated iconic watches like the Rolex Hulk, Pepsi, and Daytona, as well as powerful portraits like The Girl in the Mirror — each of them built from thousands of LEGO tiles.

But The White Horse is special. It’s the first official painting signed with number 01 — the first true Brickasso.

Beyond LEGO, I’m also passionate about music — I play the piano — and I’m curious about science, space, and how the world works. I love learning new things in school, especially math, physics, and chemistry. Every idea I have, every project I build, is part of the same mission: to create, understand, and express.

Brickasso is not just a nickname. It’s my way of showing that even as a kid, you can build something meaningful — with passion, patience, and imagination.

And this is just the beginning.
