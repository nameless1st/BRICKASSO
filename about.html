Meet the Artist – Albert Oprea

Albert Oprea is a passionate LEGO artist from Romania who began creating art from bricks at the age of 10. Inspired by iconic watches like the Rolex Hulk, Daytona, and Pepsi, as well as legendary painters like Picasso, he brings vibrant works to life with thousands of LEGO pieces.

His love for LEGO started at the age of 4 with dozens of original sets. He’d build, unbuild, and mix them, eventually creating his own art out of the chaos. What began as play has evolved into Brickasso – a project rooted in creativity, storytelling, and precision.

Today, Albert’s creations include LEGO watches, artistic portraits, and iconic cultural tributes such as the Afghan Girl by Steve McCurry. He continues to push the boundaries of LEGO as a medium of modern artistic expression.
