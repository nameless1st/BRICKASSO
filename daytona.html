Brickasso Registry: Journey #03

Daytona – LEGO® 3D Mosaic

“Daytona” celebrates precision and motion. Every sub-dial, index and reflection is rebuilt through layered LEGO® structure, balancing mechanics with artistry. The composition captures the discipline of watchmaking while expressing the freedom of sculpture.

Set against a Novacolor ARCHI+ Big (Metallo Fuso) surface and enclosed in a solid wood frame, the piece radiates an industrial elegance that connects time, craft, and architecture.

Brickasso is an independent artistic reinterpretation and is not affiliated with Rolex SA, nor endorsed or sponsored by any watch brand.
