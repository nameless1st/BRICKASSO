Brickasso Registry: #03

Girl at the Mirror

Inspired by Picasso’s masterpiece, this 3D mosaic reinvents the eternal dialogue of reflection through over 32,000 LEGO® tiles. Vivid contrasts, geometric fragments and tactile layers transform the original into a sculptural meditation on identity. Set against Novacolor ARCHI+ Big, Metallo Fuso finish, the work gains a contemporary material depth, while the solid wood frame makes it resonate as both homage and reinvention. A bridge between classic modernism and playful construction.

Inspired by Pablo Picasso’s Girl at the Mirror (1932). Brickasso’s LEGO® 3D mosaic is an independent reinterpretation and not connected to the Picasso Estate or Foundation. This artwork is not for sale and is presented solely as a personal, educational, and artistic project.
