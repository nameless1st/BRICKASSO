Certificate of Authenticity

Each Brickasso artwork is a one-of-a-kind creation, meticulously handcrafted by Albert Oprea using only original LEGO® bricks.

To guarantee the uniqueness and provenance of every piece, collectors receive an official Certificate of Authenticity — individually signed, numbered, and verified by the artist himself.

The certificate includes:

• Title of the work
• Total brick count
• Hours of creation
• Signature and date
• Unique identifier with QR code validation

For special commissions and limited editions, the certificate may also include exclusive insights about the creative process and hidden design elements.

Each certificate is printed on premium archival-grade paper and accompanied by a digitally verifiable record — ensuring authenticity for generations to come.

"Every brick tells a story — and every certificate preserves it."
— Albert Oprea
