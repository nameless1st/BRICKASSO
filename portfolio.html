Short Bio

Albert Oprea, working under the name Brickasso, uses the LEGO brick as a precise unit of color and time. He designs, sorts, and places every element by hand to build one-of-one mosaics. Each artwork is signed, recorded in the BRK Registry, and issued with a QR linked Certificate of Authenticity.

Highlights

  • The White Horse, the first signed work (18,322 LEGO pieces).
  • Watch Series, Hulk, Daytona, Pepsi.
  • Girl at the Mirror, a bold tribute through a new visual language.
  • All works are one-of-one originals, hand placed, signed, and catalogued.

Artist Statement

Each LEGO brick is a unit of time and discipline. I build images that carry focus and imagination. I treat the process as serious work, from the first layout to the final signature.

Collecting and Commissions

Every Brickasso artwork is unique, signed, and recorded in the official registry. Commissions are possible for carefully selected subjects. The process always stays one-of-one.
