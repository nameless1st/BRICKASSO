Short Bio

Albert Oprea (b. 2015), working under the moniker Brickasso, treats the LEGO® brick as a calibrated unit of time, color, and intention. Working entirely by hand, he designs, sorts, and sets tens of thousands of elements to compose one-of-one mosaics. Each work is signed, catalogued in the BRK Registry, and issued with a QR-linked Certificate of Authenticity. Based in Romania with a global outlook, he builds a body of work oriented toward demanding international platforms.

Highlights

  • The White Horse, the first signed work (18,322 LEGO® pieces).
  • Watch Series, Hulk, Daytona, Pepsi.
  • Girl at the Mirror, an homage to Picasso through a new visual alphabet.
  • All works are one-of-one originals, hand-placed, signed and catalogued.

Artist Statement

“Each LEGO® brick is a unit of time, patience, and dream. Through them, I build images that carry discipline and imagination. My art is not about LEGO®, but about what LEGO® can become when it speaks the language of serious work.”

Collecting & Commissions

Every Brickasso artwork is unique, signed, and catalogued in the official registry. Commissions are possible for carefully selected subjects, always respecting the one-of-one principle and the brick-by-brick process.
