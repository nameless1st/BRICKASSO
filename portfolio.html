Short Bio

Albert Oprea, working under the name Brickasso®, creates one-of-one sculptural artworks using original LEGO® elements as a controlled medium of color, depth and structure. Each work is built entirely by hand and exists as a unique, signed and registered artwork.

Selected Highlights

  • The White Horse, the first signed work and foundation of the Brickasso® body of work.
  • Afghan Girl, a large-scale portrait defined by density, precision and visual intensity.
  • The Perfection, a monumental artwork dedicated to Nadia Comăneci and the idea of a perfect moment.
  • David Beckham, a portrait work structured through layered detail and narrative composition.
  • Ballerina and The Black Horse, works extending the practice through scale, contrast and presence.
  • Watch Series, iconic subjects translated into one-of-one sculptural compositions.

Artist Statement

Each work is built through repetition, control and precision. Thousands of individual LEGO® elements form a single coherent structure. From a distance, the viewer sees the image. Up close, the viewer discovers miniature stories, depth and hidden details.

Collecting and Commissions

Each Brickasso® work is a unique object, signed and recorded in the official Brickasso Registry. Commissions are developed selectively, maintaining the one-of-one nature of the practice.

Technical Notes

  • Constructed exclusively from original LEGO® elements.
  • Built by hand, piece by piece.
  • Each work is accompanied by a signed Certificate of Authenticity with QR verification.
  • Works are registered under a unique BRK identification code.
  • Prepared for international transport using protective, museum grade packaging.
