Brickasso Registry: #02

Rolex Pepsi

This Brickasso 3D mosaic translates the legendary red-blue bezel into a sculptural field of LEGO® tiles, arranged with watchmaker precision. Micro-gradients, crisp indices and layered highlights create depth that shifts with the light. The scene sits on Novacolor ARCHI+ Big with a Metallo Fuso finish—an artisanal plaster whose mineral grain and fused-metal glint give the piece a contemporary, architectural presence. Mounted in a solid wood frame, the work balances technical discipline with playful materiality: a modern homage to an icon.

Brickasso is an independent artistic reinterpretation and is not affiliated with Rolex SA, nor endorsed or sponsored by any watch brand.
