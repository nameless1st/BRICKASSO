Brickasso Registry: #01

The White Horse – LEGO® 3D Mosaic

“The White Horse” was the first Brickasso creation, a declaration of courage and belief in a new form of art. Built from 18,322 LEGO® bricks, the horse stands as a symbol of strength, freedom, and trust. Every contour of mane and muscle was shaped to capture motion within stillness, and determination within silence.

Set against a Novacolor ARCHI+ Big background, the surface carries architectural depth and light, while the solid wood frame elevates it to a timeless presence. This inaugural piece marks the beginning of the Brickasso artistic journey, where structure and emotion find balance through discipline and imagination.
