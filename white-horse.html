Brickasso Registry: #01

The White Horse

Brickasso’s first official registry work, #01, the White Horse emerges from 18,322 LEGO® bricks as a living tapestry of patience and imagination. Each gradient of mane and muscle is sculpted with meticulous layering, giving motion to stillness. Displayed on Novacolor ARCHI+ Big (Metallo Fuso), the background shimmers with architectural weight, elevating the presence of the piece. Enclosed in a solid wood frame, the White Horse stands as a declaration: the beginning of a new artistic journey.

  • Registry No. BRK-01
  • Medium LEGO® bricks (mosaic)
  • Dimensions 124 x 124 cm
  • Brick count 18322
  • Hours to create 250
  • Year 2025
