David Beckham Portrait – LEGO® 3D Mosaic

I built this portrait as a tribute to discipline, focus, and the years behind a single moment. I wanted to capture more than a face. I wanted to capture a story, built from choices, pressure, and calm.

Each LEGO® piece carries intent. The surface becomes a map of light and shadow. The closer you look, the more the portrait changes, from structure to emotion, from pixels to presence.
