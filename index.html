Welcome to the world of Brickasso®

Brickasso® is the artistic practice of Albert Oprea, producing one-of-one sculptural works from LEGO® elements. Each piece is constructed entirely by hand, following an original composition and developed without digital automation. The works exist as unique objects, signed, catalogued and accompanied by a private certificate of authenticity.
